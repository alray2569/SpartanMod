What is SpartanMod?

SpartanMod is a mod for Minecraft 1.7.10 and Minecraft 1.8.2 that adds feathered leather caps and metal helmets with plumes. These are crafted by adding a feather to a vanilla Minecraft helmet in a crafting table. They do not provide any advantages over their vanilla counterparts; this change is entirely cosmetic. For those of you who wanted those Greco-Roman plumes on your helmets, wait no more.

About

SpartanMod is a Minecraft mod which I started developing in September 2015 at the request of a friend (spartanmine11 on Minecraft) who remarked that feathered hats should be available in Minecraft. I said "why not?" and so I wrote SpartanMod v1.0 on a Friday morning when I had nothing else to do.
